About InterLink

InternLink was made for students and fresh graduates who often struggle to find the right internship opportunities. Instead of using live data, it currently works with custom curated data, since live API access was not available at the time.

The aim is to provide a platform where students can explore relevant opportunities in a structured way. InternLink is designed to act as a helpful companion in the career journey, saving time and effort while offering a smooth experience.
